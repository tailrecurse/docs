## Scanning Tokens

To trade any token, simply send its mint address or symbol to the bot. The bot will:

1. **Scan the token** and display information
2. **Show trading options**: Swap, Limit Order, or DCA
3. **Display your holdings** if you own the token

### `/start`

- Creates your wallet (if first time)
- Shows the main menu
- Displays your wallet address and balances

### `/menu`

Shows the main menu.

### `/swap`

Scan a token to swap. You can:

- Send a token mint address or symbol
- The bot will scan the token and show trading options
- Choose to buy or sell tokens instantly

### `/limit`

Create a limit order. You can:

- Set buy orders at a target price below current market
- Set sell orders at a target price above current market
- Orders execute automatically when price reaches your target

### `/dca`

Set up Dollar Cost Averaging. You can:

- Automate recurring token purchases
- Set the amount per transaction
- Configure the interval (minutes, hours, or days)
- Spread your investment over time
