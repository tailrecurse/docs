### Is it safe to use?

Yes. The bot uses Privy for secure wallet management. Your private keys are encrypted and stored securely. See [Wallet Management](/trading-bot/wallet) for details.

## Trading

### How do I swap tokens?

1. Send a token mint address or symbol to the bot
2. Select "Swap" from the trading options
3. Choose to buy or sell
4. Enter the amount and confirm

### Can I cancel a limit order?

Yes, you can cancel pending limit orders before they are triggered. Once executed, the trade is complete.

### How do limit orders work?

Limit orders execute automatically when the token price reaches your target:

- **Buy orders**: Execute when price drops to your target
- **Sell orders**: Execute when price rises to your target

### What is DCA (Dollar Cost Averaging)?

DCA allows you to automate recurring token purchases at set intervals. This helps spread your investment over time and reduce the impact of price volatility.

### Can I modify or cancel a DCA order?

You can cancel active DCA orders. Once a DCA transaction executes, it cannot be reversed, but you can stop future transactions.

### How do I snipe new tokens?

When a new token launches, send its mint address to the bot and use the swap feature to buy instantly. The bot uses Jupiter aggregator for the best execution.

## Wallet & Fees

### Can I use my existing Solana wallet?

Yes. Import your wallet through Settings → Wallet → Import private key. See [Wallet Management](/trading-bot/wallet) for instructions.

### Can I export my wallet?

Yes. Go to Settings → Wallet → Export private key. See [Wallet Management](/trading-bot/wallet) for details.

### What are the transaction fees?

Very low (typically < $0.01). Fees are paid in SOL. The bot uses Jupiter aggregator which routes through the best liquidity sources.
