Fee-sharing program where you earn a percentage of trading fees from users you refer. The referral fee is calculated proportionally based on fee BPS allocation — when a referred user trades, the referral portion of the fee is tracked and becomes claimable.

## Getting an invite link

To generate your first invite link you must meet the eligibility requirements (when gating is enabled):

1. Hold a minimum of 0.1 SOL balance in your wallet.
2. Have completed at least one trade.

Once eligible, tap **Generate invite link** on the dashboard. The bot creates a unique code and displays your share link:

```
https://t.me/JupiterCometTradingBot?start=CODE
```

<Info>
  If you already have a code, the dashboard shows your link and code directly — no generation step
  needed.
</Info>

## Sharing your invite

Share by **link** or **code**. New users join by opening the link or typing `/start CODE` in the bot.

### Custom invite codes

Tap **Set Code** to pick your own code.

| Rule           | Detail                                                               |
| -------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Length**     | 3 – 64 characters.                                                   |
| **Characters** | Letters, numbers, `_`, or `-`. No spaces.                            |
| **Uniqueness** | Each code must be unique across all users. Taken codes are rejected. |

<Info>
  Renaming your code invalidates the old one. Anyone who uses the old link will no longer be
  attributed to you, and the old code may be claimed by another user.
</Info>

## Claiming fees

When claimable fees are available, the dashboard shows a wallet selector and a **Claim** button with the USD total.

1. Select the wallet to receive the payout.
2. Tap **Claim** to see a per-token breakdown of amounts.
3. Confirm to submit the on-chain transaction.

| Detail               | Value                                                          |
| -------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Tokens per claim** | Up to 5 token types per transaction. Claim again for the rest. |
| **Gas**              | Paid from your wallet — you need SOL for transaction fees.     |
| **Fee lifecycle**    | Pending (trade just executed) → Claimable → Claimed.           |

<Info>
  If a claim times out, the transaction may still confirm on-chain. Check back shortly — the bot
  recovers interrupted claims automatically.
</Info>
