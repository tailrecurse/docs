## Using the Bot in Groups

The bot works in both private chats and group chats. Groups are perfect for discovering and discussing markets together.

### Adding Bot to Group

**From `/start` command:**

- Tap the **üë• Add to Group** button that appears when you use `/start`
- Select the group you want to add it to

**From Group Settings:**

- Open the bot settings
- Click more
- Add To Group

<img
  src="/images/join-group-config.png"
  alt="Withdraw input amount screen"
  style={{ display: 'block', margin: '0 auto', maxWidth: '100%', borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
/>

### Group Features

In groups, you can:

- Browse events and markets together
- View market details and prices
- Discuss predictions with your group

**Placing Bets:**

- When you find a market in a group, tap "Bet in Private Chat"
- A private chat opens automatically
- Complete your bet securely in the private chat

<Info>
  Betting happens in private chats to keep your wallet and transactions secure. You can still
  discover markets with your group!
</Info>
