Clans let you compete with friends and track your group's performance.

## Using the Bot in Groups

The bot works in both private chats and group chats. Groups are perfect for discovering and discussing markets together.

### Adding Bot to Group

**From `/start` or `/menu` command:**

- Tap the **👥 Add to Group** button that appears
- Select the group you want to add it to

**From Group Settings:**

- Open the bot settings
- Click more
- Add To Group

## Event Resolved Notifications

When an entire event is resolved, you'll see a leaderboard of all participants:

- **Winners**: Listed with their positive PnL
- **Losers**: Listed with their negative PnL
- Your clan's current standings
