## Market Resolved Notifications

When markets you've predicted on are resolved, you'll receive a detailed summary showing:

- Each position's outcome (✅ won or ❌ lost)
- Your PnL (profit/loss) for each position
- Links to view the claim transaction and the event

<img
  src="/images/prediction-bot/market_resolved_notification.png"
  alt="Market resolved notification showing position outcomes"
  style={{ display: 'block', margin: '0 auto', maxWidth: '100%', borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
/>

## Automatic Enrollment

When you place a prediction on an event, notifications for that event's category are **automatically enabled**. You can disable them later in settings if you don't want them.
