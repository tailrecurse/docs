### How do I get started?

See the [Getting Started guide](/prediction-bot/getting-started/introduction) for step-by-step instructions.

### Is it safe to use?

Yes. The bot uses Privy for secure wallet management. Your private keys are encrypted and stored securely. See [Wallet Management](/prediction-bot/wallet) for details.

## Predictions

### Can I cancel a prediction?

You can cancel pending orders, but once a prediction becomes a position, you can only close it or sell it at the current market price.

### How do I know if I won?

You'll receive a notification when a winning position becomes claimable. See [Notifications](/prediction-bot/notifications) for details.

## Wallet & Fees

### Can I use my existing Solana wallet?

Yes. Import your wallet through Settings → Wallet → Import private key. See [Wallet Management](/prediction-bot/wallet) for instructions.

### Can I export my wallet?

Yes. Go to Settings → Wallet → Export private key. See [Wallet Management](/prediction-bot/wallet) for details.

### What are the transaction fees?

Transaction fees are covered — no SOL needed.
