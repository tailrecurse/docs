## Getting Started

### Step 1: Join the Telegram Group

To access and communicate with the bot, you must first join our private beta group:

1. [Join Comet Predict Private Beta](https://jupiter-exchange.slack.com/archives/C0A1Q5W0G06/p1764930476799409)
2. Once you've joined the group, you'll be able to access the bot

### Step 2: Start the Bot

1. [Launch the bot](https://t.me/jup_pred_bot)
2. Click **Start** or send `/start` to begin
3. The bot will automatically create a Solana wallet for you

### Step 3: Wallet Setup

The bot automatically creates a Solana wallet for you when you first start using the bot in a **private chat**.

Your wallet address is displayed in the main menu. You can:

- **Copy the address** to receive funds from exchanges or other wallets
- **View your balances** (SOL and USDC) directly in the menu
- **Export your private key** if you want to use the wallet elsewhere
- **Import your wallet** in case you prefer

### Step 4: Fund Your Wallet

Before you can place bets, you need to fund your wallet with SOL and USDC:

1. **Copy your wallet address** from the main menu
2. **Send SOL and USDC** to this address:

<Info>**Important**: Keep some SOL in your wallet for transaction fees.</Info>
